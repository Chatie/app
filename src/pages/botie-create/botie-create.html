What is a Botie?

A Botie is the configuration for your ChatBot. It's the right place to setup what your bot say when it receives new message, and what Giftie you want to install.

More technic:

  1. Botie is like package.json for NPM.
  2. Botie can install many Gifties and put them together as a group. Those Gifties is the Functions that you want your bot behavior.
